# Guidelines - Final Paper

Your goal is to construct a paper that would be viable with some work for a presentation for a shorter networking conference (e.g. IEEE GLOBECOM or IEEE ICC). You should plan on using LaTeX and using a tool such as Overleaf can make the process much easier.

General guidelines for the paper are as follows:

* IEEE style meaning two column, either 10 or 11 point font
* Single blind meaning that your names should be visible
* Five to eight pages inclusive of references
* At least ten references - eight of which should be peer reviewed
   * Think papers that appear in a respectable venue with an appropriate peer review process, e.g. not a generic website link, blog post, or industry report.  arXiV papers are not peer reviewed and would fall into this same category.
* At least one broader system framework view that captures your overall approach
* At least two graphs and / or tables of results
