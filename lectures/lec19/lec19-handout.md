# Lecture 19 - Handout - 03-30-26 - Grad Networks

## Overview

In the last lecture, we discussed the two surveys about NDN and ICN, namely shifting away from a host-centric view to that of a data-centric view.  In today's lecture, we will look at sensor networks, a field that emerged in the early part of the 2000's as wireless connectivity afforded the ability for mobile sensing.  Sensing and low-power devices introduced a host of challenges and opportunities as discussed in the survey paper that we will discuss today.  In today's lecture, we will go through the survey paper and discuss more broadly how the Internet of Things (IoT) and Cyber Physical Systems (CPS) follow along those same lines.
