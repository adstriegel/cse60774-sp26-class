# Schedule for ND CSE 60774 - Spring Semester

## First Half

The first half of the class will consist of four modules:

* **Module 1: Introduction:** An introductory module covering key aspects of undergraduate networks with a broader focus on building / reinforcing concepts relevant to the concepts covered later in the course.
* **Module 2: Congestion Control:** A focus on the evolution of TCP with a specific focus on modern Congestion Control Algorithms (CCAs) including CUBIC and BBR
* **Module 3: Quality of Service / Quality of Experience:** An examination of how QoS evolved into QoE and how instrumentation is undertaken to evaluate QoS / QoE within a network
* **Module 4: Wireless:** Fundamentals of how WiFi and cellular have evolved as exploring ad hoc networking

## Second Half

The second half will focus on applied aspects of networking at scale.

* **Module 5: Content Distribution:** Routing, DNS, ICN / NDN
* **Module 6: Cyber-Physical Systems:** Sensor networks, Internet of Things, edge computing, vehicular networks, interim student presentations
* **Module 7: Contemporary Topics:** Software defined networking (SDN), datacenter networking, mesh networking
* **Module 8: Project Presentations:** Final project presentations
