# Lecture 24 - Handout - 04-20-26 - Grad Networks

## Overview

In the last lecture, we looked at Software Defined Networks (SDN) exploring the roots and history of SDN as well as one of the earlier pre-cursors to SDN. In today's lecture, we will look at various network security devices with an eye towards IPS (Intrusion Prevention Systems) / IDS (Intrusion Detection Systems) as well as how various network security devices scale.  The paper today will take a look back at one of the original IDS papers, Bro, which is still quite relevant today and discuss how it is related to SDN and security.
