# Lecture 23 - Handout - 04-15-26 - Grad Networks

## Overview

In Monday's lecture, we wrapped up the content on sensor networks, concluding with a discussion of the various problems centered around vehicular networks.  We explored DSRC and 802.11p as well as why / how different scenarios impact the various solutions.  In today's lecture, we will pivot to the last module, examining the back end and security.  We will focus today on Software Defined Networking, exploring one of the early works on the road to SDN and then examining a perspective paper that discusses the road toward SDN. After SDN, we will focus largely on security, examining first IDS / IPS next Monday.
