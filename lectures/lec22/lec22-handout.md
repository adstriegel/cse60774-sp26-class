# Lecture 22 - Handout - 04-13-26 - Grad Networks

## Overview

With the interim presentations complete from last Wednesday, we will now be pivoting into the last portions of the course.  We have one more lecture focusing on constrained networks (vehicular networks) before we pivot into the back end (SDN, precursors to SDN) and security.  Interestingly, vehicular networks tend to operate on a bit of a different timescale due to the longevity of vehicles (10+ years) from a deployment standpoint relative to many other forms of technology with a shorter upgrade cycle.  We will compare / contrast the unique challenges of vehicular networks which also has some parallels to UAVs versus the more fixed sensor networks from the past lectures.
